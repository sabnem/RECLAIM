Terms & Conditions

By using the Lost & Found Portal, you agree to follow these guidelines to keep our community safe and helpful for everyone.

General Guidelines

  • Only report items you have genuinely lost or found.
  • Respect other users and communicate honestly.
  • Do not share sensitive personal information.
  • Return items promptly and confirm returns as required.
  • Violations may result in account suspension or removal.

Safe Meeting Practices

  • Arrange meetings in well-lit, public locations.
  • Bring a friend or inform someone about your meeting.
  • Verify the identity of the other party before exchanging items.
  • Trust your instincts and cancel if you feel unsafe.
Need Help?

If you have questions or concerns about our policies, please contact support.
